##IBM HR Analytics Dashboard
#Project Overview:
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and employee trends.
The dashboard helps HR teams and management understand employee turnover patterns and identify key factors influencing attrition. It provides actionable insights through interactive visualizations and KPI metrics.

#Business Problem:
Employee attrition can significantly impact an organization's productivity and hiring costs. The objective of this project is to analyze employee data and identify patterns related to employee turnover, salary, age groups, departments, and overtime work.

#Tools used:
1.Power BI,
2.Power Query,
3.DAX (Data Analysis Expressions),and
4.Excel Dataset(IBM HR Analytics(Kaggle dataset)).

#Data Preparation:
The dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query.

#Data Cleaning Steps:
Removed unnecessary columns from dataset:
>EmployeeCount
>EmployeeNumber
>Over18
>StandardHours
Checked for missing values and data quality issues

Created custom analytical columns:
>Attrition Flag
>Age Group
>Income Group
>Experience Level

#DAX Measures Created:
1.Total Employees
2.Employees Left
3.Attrition Rate
4.Employees Left with Overtime
5.Average Salary
6.Average Salary of Employees Left

#Dashboard Features:
#KPI Cards:
>Total Employees
>Employees Left
>Attrition Rate
>Employees Left with Overtime
>Average Salary
>Average Salary of Employees Left

#Interactive Filters:
>Department
>Gender
>Age Group
>Income Group

#Visualizations:
>Attrition by Department
>Attrition by Job Role
>Attrition by Gender
>Attrition by Overtime
>Employees by Age Group
>Attrition by Income Group

#Key Insights:
Research & Development department has the highest employee attrition.
Laboratory Technicians show the highest turnover among job roles.
More than 50% of employees who left the company worked overtime.
Low-income employees experience higher attrition compared to other income groups.
Mid-Career employees form the largest workforce segment.
